The Data

We made some discoveries of our own in the data to help you get a better understanding of AirBNB in San Francisco


From the data we see that the price of AirBNBs tend to be cheaper toward the outskirts of the city (see the log price graph) but that those outskirts also tend to have more beds (see the rightmost plot about beds in San Francisco AirBNBs). If you are a family looking for a affordable housing - we recommend the city limits! Generally cheaper housing with more room is the way to go. We can also tell my the middle graph that AirBNBs in San Francisco tend to filled more often in the center of the city - our graph here only colors AirBNBs that are booked more than 75% of the time. You can see for yourself that a large fraction of the colors lie in the heart of the city. Lesson being - if you want to stay downtown, book early!
